Breathe Deeper

How clean is your air?

A webapp to display the air quality over the USA and the population density by state. Press 'Start' and coloured dots relating to the Air Quality Index will appear on the map in areas that supply the data. Hovering within a state boundry displays that states name and the pop. density of people per square mile. The colour dots relate to:

0-25 AQI, Almost or completely clean air

25-50 AQI, Satisfactory air quality

50-100 AQI, May effect extremely sensitive groups

100-150 AQI, Members of sensitive groups affected

150-200 AQI, General public risk health concerns

200-250 AQI, Entire pop. face serious health risks

250-300 AQI, Just dont go outside
